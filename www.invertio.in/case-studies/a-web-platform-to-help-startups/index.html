Project Synopsis

One of the biggest product-based organizations in India partnered with startups to help them grow into world-class organizations, and they contacted invertio to create their digital assets. This helped establish them as the most preferred platform among employees of startups and also helped various organizations worldwide to scale meaningfully into world-class companies.

The project is to provide best quality assurance for the People Cues tool, a simple and user-friendly tool to implement and manage OKRs effectively. A feature-rich, user-friendly, and visually appealing application for the web which brings transparency, objectivity, and fun to employee recognition and completely transforms the experience for employees.

  • Popular amongst 70,000+ employees across 200+ organizations and counting
  • Seamless user experience with an engaging & interactive interface
  • Completely configurable performance reviews management

Collaborating with invertio, the client expected a feature-rich, user-friendly and interactive web app which drives extreme clarity in achieving org-wide targets. The user-friendly tool is well complemented with unrelenting support from experienced professionals.

Getting familiar with the layout

Depending on subscription type user should see a combination of the following links on the navigation bar on the left side.

  • Home is a consolidated view of statuses and to-dos. See Getting Started on Home.
  • Objectives: Create and manage your company and personal goals from the Objectives module. See Getting started on Objectives.
  • Reviews: Create and manage your performance review cycles. See Getting Started on Reviews.
  • Feedback: Give and solicit continuous and real-time feedback from your peers. See Getting Started on Feedback.
  • Surveys: Measure your organization’s workforce sentiment through carefully crafted templates or create your own custom survey. See Getting Started with Surveys.
  • AMA: Engage with your internal teams through the Ask Me Anything (AMA) module.
  • Settings: Invite users to PeopleCues and configure the tool to your specific requirements through the Settings page.
  • What’s new: Stay up-to-date with all the latest feature releases in the product.
  • Help: Still can’t find what you are looking for? Reach out to us from the Help section.

Solution Implementation

Agile Development Process

A team of expert product managers, UI/UX designers, developers and database managers brainstormed to put it all together under a wireframe to deliver to the client and users expectations, keeping in mind the end business goal –

  • Business Goal: Completely configurable performance reviews management – For achieving the business goal SMART technique is followed and the solution is designed based on 3 layer information processing –
  • Pre Development: Discovery of the project goals, requirements elicitation, in-depth analysis, and validation preparing a project plan
  • Development: A customized solution designed and developed catering to the specific needs of the client business, keeping the project objective & business goals in mind.
  • Post Development: Guaranteeing stability after the delivery of the app, the invertio team provides complete maintenance and support services post-release.

Documentation

The architecture design document describes the components and specifications required to support the solution and ensure that the specific business and technical requirements of the design are satisfied. The document states the business goals and the goals of the solution. The technical writer drafted the purpose of building this architecture and the vision it wishes to fulfill. This document also included the constraints that the project could expect to have, so the development team could ensure preparedness against all possible challenges.
