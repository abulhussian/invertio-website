Top 5 Countries For Offshore Mobile App Development

Are you pondering how to form a leading mobile application for your business? If yes, first find the best countries to outsource mobile app development.

Several small to big businesses outsource mobile app development to optimize costs. Besides being a cost-effective solution, outsourcing is one of the topmost ways to draw a large pool of skilled and talented professionals for your mobile application development project.

Have a look at the following list of the best countries for outsourcing application development:

1) India

It is one of the best countries to outsource app development. Moreover, India is the second-largest English-speaking country in the globe, making it an ideal alternative for software outsourcing.

Comparatively, the country has a low literacy level, but the government pays attention to mathematical research and the advancement of technologies. India’s STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) graduates number is 2.6 million, which is quite enormous and is still increasing by the passing years.

India is one of the prime locations for software outsourcing as it grants affordable and even you can say the low cost of developers. Average mobile app developers in India charge between $15 to $25/hr, making them less costly than mobile application development firms in the US that usually charge an average of $60/hr.

Hence, if you are having a low budget for mobile app development, you can think of hiring mobile developers working in the top mobile application development companies in India.
