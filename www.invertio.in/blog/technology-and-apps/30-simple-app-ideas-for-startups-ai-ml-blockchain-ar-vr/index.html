The demand for new app ideas is increasing day by day, More and more businesses are building apps that are lightweight, tech-savvy, fast, and serve the purpose.

In 2017–18, there were around $197 billion of mobile app downloads. And, this will jump to a stunning mark of $352 billion by 2021. The era of complex and heavy apps is gone. Businesses today want simple mobile apps and new app ideas that can be deployed easily.

Hence, the mindset of businesses should be clear on how they’re going to build their next mobile app with a new app idea.

If you’re looking for new app ideas, no worries, here we have got 30 new app ideas for startups and SMEs to consider in 2019. Also, we have divided these new app ideas into different categories i.e. Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, and Augmented Reality & Virtual Reality.

10 Blockchain-Enabled New App Ideas for Startups and SMEs 

The blockchain is making its’s market very fast. By 2020, it will revamp the financial industry totally. Hence, here are a few new app ideas on Blockchain development.

1) New App Idea for Money Lending & Borrowing 

In today’s world, both borrowers and lenders are connected in the digital world via blockchain. With the money lending apps such as smart contracts, all of the consumers can perform financial transactions without including the bank or any other financial institution.

The blockchain-based app ideas help you remain completely anonymous and also there is no need to register anywhere. Another benefit is borrowers can select their preferred loan medium from the marketplace and can return the loan in a safe and low-interest environment.

2) Digital Wills

Today myriad of people go for cryptocurrency development to save their funds, but how can you prevent a loss if you die without a will? You can use a platform like My wish built on smart contracts and can handle your funds during a crisis. This new app idea allows you to transfer money and your savings to your family members in the event of illness or death.

3) New App Idea for Saving Electricity Bills

This new app idea help monitors your power usage and helps save cost on your electricity bills. The app’s idea is to monitor the power usage by using solidity blockchain technology that helps many people in saving the power bills they get via a meter.

4) Storing Medical Records

Today, doctors and health professionals are limited in providing the best level of medicare care to patients because of their inability to track patients’ health records. The new idea of healthcare application development is based on the multichain blockchain technology that allows patients to enter their medical records safely on a decentralized network. It is also useful for doctors because it allows them to access patients’ data from a remote place.

Let’s see an example, the Estonia government in collaboration with the leading blockchain technology company named Guardtime planning built a platform blockchain of citizen’s health records. The goal is to track patient health records in real-time by using the ID credentials that allow them to unlock the patients’ medical data in a completely safe manner.

5) App Idea for the Real Estate Industry

It is really difficult to manage and maintain the registry of land ownership and real-estate-related operations. In such a scenario, Ubitquity is the platform built on the ERC blockchain where you can keep your real estate records safely. It simply reduces the need for title search time and also improves transparency in real estate operations.

6) Gift Portals

There are many gifts portal that uses the traditional form of coupons and plastic tokens to send discounts and loyalty rewards to their customers. But, today, we have a platform like Rouge that allows businesses to create their own ICO Blockchain-based loyalty tokens and offer rewards to their customers.

7) Entertainment Industry 

Ownership rights and transparency are the main issues in the music and entertainment industry. Blockchain technology can solve this problem by using a comprehensive and decentralized database of your media content rights. At the same time, transparency in the transmission of artist royalties is maintained throughout the distribution process and this is why it is on our list of new app ideas for the entertainment industry.

8) New App Idea for Identity 

The best example of blockchain for identity was a digital passport launched on Github in the year 2014 this could help a person identify themselves online. To use this platform you need to take your picture, now get it stamped with the option of public and private keys to make it legitimate. After this, your digital passport is saved on the ledger, and you can access it by entering the Bitcoin address with a public IP as confirmed by the users. Isn’t it a great app idea for businesses looking to add more security options to their website? So, to turn your app idea into reality, you need to hire blockchain developers who are the best in the industry.

9) Transport Industry

Uber is the best platform to book cars and taxis for rent. But, what if they decide to pull out of a city? Maybe thousands of drivers become jobless and passengers will have no options to book cars and taxis for rent. If you wish to add a new app idea to your transportation business, then let’s see an example of the Arcade City car ride app that beat Uber with its ride-sharing and car hiring platform that is entirely based on Ethereum blockchain development.

This is the platform where both riders and drivers are connected directly to earn the Arcade car ride tokens. Their network has proved a safe and reliable mode of transportation in all the major U.S cities for over a year. So, what’s your view about this app idea?

10) New App Idea for Web Hosting 

You need a web hosting service when your site is down due to server overload. Our new app idea is based on the Stellar blockchain development and to provide you with a better platform where your web content does not require to rely on any central server. Like you can go for Zeronet which creates a decentralized connection to host your business website on servers by storing your website information across the web in small quantities. This platform reduces the cost of web hosting and is also easily accessible to users.

10 AI & ML New App Ideas for Startups and SMEs

Artificial Intelligence is not new. However, this time it is growing very fast and reducing human efforts in many industries. Here are a few new app ideas based on AI/ML.

1) New App Idea for Retail Brands

Do you have an e-commerce site? If yes, you need to add unique and new photos for your products, and most of the time you have to search through Google or social media to find photos. Instead of this, it would be better to use an on-device recognition AI/ML app like IntelliP that scan, organize, and categorize all kind of images in your smartphone based on the items given in the image.

Our new app idea is beneficial for businesses that wish to obtain an incredible range of photos relevant to their geographical location, product type, and potential markets.

2) New App Idea for Journalists

Journalists need to create stories about the latest events. It takes a lot of effort for respondents to collect data and information. The most effective way is to provide a journalist with the real AI/ML experience that auto-generates reports by asking all the vital details from you via voice conversation that a journalist can review later. Isn’t it a great app idea for journalists? Yes, it is!  It could help you generate new questions based on the previous reviews, help you identify similar reports, and provide merged versions of the report.

3) Geo Tracking 

Our new app idea is based on AI & ML geo-tracking that would be a perfect choice for businesses that need to search for potential points of interest, stores, supermarkets, spas, party venues, and more places.

Our new app idea is inspired by the GeoInterest apps that operate on your phone and guide you through detecting and locating different places of interest determined by you on a trip.

4) New App Idea for Writers 

Are you a writer? Have you ever used a tool that can add new content to your digital diary by simply speaking to it? If not, trust me, you need to have it if you haven’t. Our new app idea for writers is to eliminate the use of your pen and/or hands to write new stories, documents, and newsletters. Sounds great!

The “NaturalYou” is one such cool app idea that would allow you to add new content to your diary by simply speaking to it. It simply eliminates the need of keeping your hands busy in translating the words and allows your thoughts to get quickly recorded as you speak them out.

Apps based on AI/ML can be great for businesses operating in the banking and finance industry. These apps help analyze your payment transaction history and offer you what you want in terms of online payment, transparency, and cybersecurity. This is our new app idea for the finance industry that can be used to manage the financial expenditure of a customer by linking their credit cards and accounts.

Erica is the best example of an ML-enabled virtual voice assistant that helps keep your finances safe in the digital space and definitely is on the list of great and new app ideas for banking institutions and finance companies.

Our AI and ML new app idea for weather forecasting will take your current location to give you the accurate forecasting of climate in a particular region. 7) Food Delivery

Our next new app idea is for businesses from food delivery that can resolve your headache of taking orders, managing orders, resolving customers’ queries, and getting the best suggestions for recipes according to your customer preference.

8) App Idea for Healthcare Industry 

Machine learning apps in healthcare can play a big role in giving you a quick analysis of the symptoms of your health condition. For example, there are apps based on AI/ML that can detect the possibility of a headache and can suggest ways to prevent it. These apps have features like sensors that can analyze the health data and provide users with a particular medication, and workout programs to nurture their well-being.

The one new ML app idea is Freeletics which designs your workout plan based on your level of workout history.

The rapid growth of electronic health records has improved the accuracy of data records and reduced data errors, for example, duplicate data. The objective of healthcare application development is to create safe and easily accessible applications that benefit users.

The automated AI/ML technology in the travel industry can help users in planning their trips and future voyages as quickly as possible. 10 AR and VR New App Ideas for Startups and SMEs

AR/VR is one of the newest trends that are reshaping industries. Be it games or education there are some major impacts of these technologies. Here I have articulated some of the new app ideas based on AR/VR.

1) Landscape Designing 

Companies often look to use AR and VR app ideas in their landscape design. So, they can go for our new app idea. ARKit is one such app with useful features. It helps in the fast and easy landscape measurements using Apple devices. The app can also give you automatic cost calculations of your landscaping space. It has the ability to show you a 3D model of the proposed landscape design.

2) New App Idea for AR/VR Learning

Our new app idea for learning is inspired by the app named Mondly which is one of the best AR and VR apps to learn a new language that you can use on your phone and you can learn on the go. You can choose over 33 different languages for quick and easy learning in no time at all. Isn’t it a cool app idea for all e-learners out there?

3) New App Idea for Construction Repair Sites 

Many times technicians on the construction sites encounter situations where they need to provide quick support and repair service. Our new app idea can be of great help in all such situations. You can implement this app idea by adding training modules on rare machinery and equipment. One app that has inspired us is Metaio’s which offers an AR-based repair tool and there are countless apps to be developed by the VR/AR developers in the industry.

4) Shopping Sites

When you buy a new piece of furniture for your home, it happens that it doesn’t fit your home space or it doesn’t go with the décor of your house. Now, what will you do? Do you replace it to make it work? No, simply go for the IKEA app that let you try out their range of furniture in any room. Yes, it’s true. And, this is our new app idea where you only need to use your phone camera to see whatever furniture you like virtually. It also allows you to discover the range of furniture in various colors and styles.

5) New App Idea for AR teleconferencing 

If the idea of sitting in a boardroom for another status update makes you unhappy, then our AR and VR new app idea is the best fit for you. Our idea is to give you an app that can curate complete details of the boardroom. So, you could also walk through each component virtually. AR teleconferencing is all about reaching distant people. But today, it is also bringing the whole world near you, so that you can understand things in a better way. And, this is our new app idea based on AR/VR technologies.

6) Construction Site Management

AR and VR in the construction industry help project developers take a glance at buildings in 3D format. Like, you can use the new platform of Apple AR that offers a great deal for real estate developers. Our new app idea will allow you to use anchors and tracking features to scan the construction site on your iPhone. Here are a few examples of how the AR and VR apps can be used for site management:

All the electrical lines and plumbing lines on your construction site will appear where they will be placed using only the Apple iPhone or iPad.

ARKit tool allows applications to provide a complete analysis of a construction site in real-time by scanning the project site.

7) New App Idea for Interior Designers 

With our new app idea of AR/VR for interior designing, you can style any room in the desired layout before you even buy a single piece of furniture. And this is possible by using your smartphone or iPhone camera, you can take a look around your living room, bedroom, kitchen, office, or any other place that you want to furnish and can also place different items in 3D mode to see how they look virtually.

8) Building Maintenance

The AR/VR technology makes it possible for you to take a record of all the important data. It is for the maintenance of a building site. With our new VR app idea, you can access the display of the location of water and gas pipes. This would be around and inside the building walls. The app can also give you a complete analysis of elevators shafts and service panels. Hence, you will be getting accurate directions in most buildings.

If you want to know how many bulbs are there in your building, then our AR/VR app idea provides you with a scanner on the shelf. It can show you complete details of bulbs placed in a building.

9) New App Idea for Marketing Specialists 

Our new AR/VR app idea will open up a new world of possibilities for brands and businesses. Users can get the full analysis of market reports, market forecasts, and the current market stats in virtual form. Also, with the details of the rest of the information relevant to marketing.

Take a general example, when you hand your children a smartphone in a shopping cart. The entire e-store becomes an amusement platform for your kids filled with games to play and prizes that they can win. So, this is how AR and VR apps capitalize on the needs of their captive audience.

10) AR and VR App Idea for Healthcare Professionals

There are many useful new healthcare application development ideas for doctors and physicians who’re using AR/VR technologies to check an individual’s anatomy in a 3D-enabled environment. With AR and VR apps like AccuVein, Holo Eye Anatomy, and Touch Surgery doctors can get a complete walk-through of the patient’s anatomy from different 3D perspectives.
