WhatsApp is moving forward with exciting new AI features that users will love. These include generating images and a helpful chat powered by Meta AI.

WhatsApp, the most widely used messaging app globally owned by Meta, is stepping into the future of messaging with its introduction of artificial intelligence (AI) features.

Currently, WhatsApp is trying out its initial AI features in India and South Africa. These will probably be available worldwide soon.

The Meta AI chatbot has been tested with a few users since November 2023. Now, it’s being rolled out to more people.

A spokesperson from Meta said, “We are currently developing generative AI-powered experiences at different stages, and we are testing several of them with the public in a limited way.”

In the testing phase for private WhatsApp users in India and South Africa, two key new functions enabled by WhatsApp AI are:

  • Creating AI images within WhatsApp chats.
  • Chatting with Meta AI.

Here’s all you need to understand about the Meta new chatbot WhatsApp AI tools and how they can make your WhatsApp experience better.

Mark Zuckerberg, the CEO of Meta, has emphasized the strategic significance of WhatsApp, describing it as the ‘next big step’ for Meta and a possible foundation for business messaging.

How to Use WhatsApp AI?

Here, we explain the two main features: generating AI images in WhatsApp and chatting with Meta AI. Both are easy. Just follow these steps:

How to create an AI image in WhatsApp

One of the most thrilling additions is the AI-driven image creation in WhatsApp. Hire AI engineers to add this feature. It lets users design special images right within their chats, injecting creativity into conversations and adding a playful touch to everyday messaging

1. Start a chat: Select a contact or group to send the AI-generated image to.

2. Access the feature: Tap on the attachment icon (paperclip), then choose ‘AI Image’ from the menu.

3. Describe the image: Type in a description of the image you’d like to create, such as “a modern city skyline during sunset.”

4. Create and send: Tap ‘Create’, and once the image is generated, you can send it directly in your chat.

How to chat with Meta AI (@AI is all you need to know)

Here’s how to call upon the Meta AI chatbot (powered by Meta’s own LLaMA – more details below):

Start the AI chat: In any chat, simply type “@AI” followed by your question or prompt.
Interact naturally: Have a conversation as you normally would. The AI can offer explanations, and suggestions, and even engage in a friendly chat.
Pose questions: Seek assistance for tasks such as setting reminders, retrieving information, or translating languages directly within your WhatsApp chat.

Meta LLaMA Powers WhatsApp AI

How does WhatsApp incorporate AI into its app?

This is achievable through Meta’s LLaMA (Language Model Meta AI) tool.

What is LLaMA?

LLaMA, which stands for Language Model Meta AI, is an advanced language processing AI created by Meta. It’s built to comprehend and produce text similar to humans, allowing for more intricate and nuanced interactions between users and AI on WhatsApp.

How does LLaMA improve WhatsApp?

Improved Interaction: LLaMA enhances the AI’s capability to grasp context and reply in conversations, resulting in smoother and more natural interactions.
Multilingual Support: With LLaMA, WhatsApp’s AI can communicate in various languages, ensuring a smooth experience for users worldwide.
Personalized Responses: LLaMA can customize responses according to the conversation’s past interactions, providing personalized interaction that surpasses typical automated replies.

This integration not only demonstrates Meta’s dedication to advancing AI technology but also boosts user engagement on WhatsApp by making interactions more natural and adaptable.

With LLaMA, WhatsApp is poised to redefine communication standards in messaging apps, providing users with a more dynamic and responsive experience.
